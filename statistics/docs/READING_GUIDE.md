# Indicator notes

- `R_3`: 3-day return; captures the freshest directional move.
- `R_7`: 7-day return; shows the last week of direction.
- `R_14`: 14-day return; short swing context.
- `TS_20`: 20-day trend strength; short-term directional pressure.
- `TS_50`: 50-day trend strength; medium structure health.
- `TS_200`: 200-day trend strength; long structure bias.
- `LR_20`: 20-day regression slope; cleaner short trend estimate.
- `LR_50`: 50-day regression slope; medium trend slope.
- `LR_200`: 200-day regression slope; long trend slope.
- `ER_20`: 20-day efficiency ratio; trend cleanliness versus chop.
- `ER_50`: 50-day efficiency ratio; medium-horizon path efficiency.
- `ER_200`: 200-day efficiency ratio; long-horizon directional efficiency.
- `RVR_20`: 20-day return versus realized volatility; risk-adjusted drift.
- `RVR_50`: 50-day return versus realized volatility; medium-horizon drift quality.
- `RVR_200`: 200-day return versus realized volatility; long-horizon drift quality.
- `vol_20`: 20-day realized volatility from daily log returns.
- `atr_pct`: ATR as a share of price; practical daily movement scale.
- `parkinson_vol`: High-low volatility estimate; intraday range intensity.
- `garman_klass_vol`: Open-high-low-close volatility estimate; fuller candle-based vol.
- `ewma_vol`: Recency-weighted volatility; reacts faster to change.
- `upside_semi_vol`: Volatility of positive returns only.
- `downside_semi_vol`: Volatility of negative returns only.
- `band_w`: Rolling range width; compression versus expansion.
- `band_pos`: Location inside the rolling high-low band.
- `dist_from_mean_vol_units`: Distance from rolling mean in volatility units; stretch gauge.
- `time_since_local_high`: Days since the latest local high.
- `time_since_local_low`: Days since the latest local low.
- `body_to_range_ratio`: Candle body relative to total range; conviction of the bar.
- `upper_wick_ratio`: Upper wick share of range; rejection near highs.
- `lower_wick_ratio`: Lower wick share of range; rejection near lows.
- `close_in_range`: Where the close finished inside the candle range.
- `run_length_up`: Length of the current up streak.
- `run_length_down`: Length of the current down streak.
- `run_magnitude_up`: Cumulative size of the current up streak.
- `run_magnitude_down`: Cumulative size of the current down streak.
- `return_accel`: Second difference of returns; momentum acceleration or fade.
- `relative_volume_20`: Volume versus its 20-day mean; participation check.
- `volume_z`: Volume deviation from its recent norm.
- `P_CORE_HMM`: Probability of the neutral/core HMM regime.
- `P_DRIFT_HMM`: Probability of the orderly positive drift regime.
- `P_SHOCK_HMM`: Probability of the stress/shock regime.
- `P_SURGE_HMM`: Probability of the strong positive expansion regime.
- `HMM_CONF`: Confidence in the dominant latent HMM state.
- `HMM_DOM`: Dominant latent HMM state index.
- `P_CORRECTION_10D_CAL`: Calibrated probability of a meaningful downside correction within 10 days.
- `P_REBOUND_10D_CAL`: Calibrated probability of a meaningful rebound within 10 days.
- `direction_safe`: SAFE directional bias used by the exposure layer.
- `E_target_safe`: SAFE target exposure.
- `L_target_safe`: SAFE target leverage or notional intensity.
- `conviction_safe`: SAFE conviction after regime and hazard modulation.
- `D_score_safe`: SAFE internal damage score; higher means weaker state.
- `hard_risk_off_flag_safe`: SAFE hard risk-off override flag.
- `ONCHAIN_VOL_Z`: On-chain activity intensity relative to recent history.
- `ONCHAIN_DOM_Z`: Whale-dominance deviation versus recent history.
- `ONCHAIN_WHALE_SHARE_Z`: Whale-share deviation versus recent history.
- `ONCHAIN_AMOUNT_PCT`: Day-over-day change in transferred amount.
- `ONCHAIN_WHALE_TX_PCT`: Day-over-day change in whale transaction count.
- `ONCHAIN_DOM_PCT`: Day-over-day change in on-chain whale dominance.
